# blackjack trainer
[Click here to start](https://noahsug.github.io/blackjack-trainer/)

UI
 - the top number is the dealer's card
 - the middle two numbers are your cards
 - H = hit
 - S = stand
 - D = double
 - P = split

Rules
 - 4+ decks
 - hit on soft 17
 - double after split
 - no surrender
 - peak for blackjack
